About Cat Genetics Simulator

The Mission

At Cat Genetics Simulator, we're passionate about feline genetics and dedicated to helping breeders, veterinarians, and cat enthusiasts understand the complex world of cat genetic inheritance.

Why?

Our simulator provides an accessible way to predict how various genetic traits may be inherited when breeding cats. By understanding genetic outcomes, breeders can make more informed decisions to promote healthy cats with desirable traits while minimizing genetic health issues.

Founded on Science

Our genetic prediction models are built on established research in feline genetics. We constantly update our algorithms based on the latest scientific findings to ensure accurate predictions for coat colors, patterns, and potential health conditions.

Educational Resource

Beyond providing predictions, we aim to educate our users about cat genetics. Each gene in our simulator comes with detailed information about how it functions and affects your cat's appearance and health.
